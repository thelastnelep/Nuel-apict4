Favorite Character

This page is about my favorite character, Radahn Consort of Miquella from one of the biggest and succesful games, Elden Ring. Elden ring a soulsborne game made by FromSoftware, is based on dark souls, if it was open world. Set in the Lands Between, this game is about the quest of the Tarnished, which is the player, becoming the new Elden Lord. The whole game is made of six main areas with dungeons, NPC quests, cities, caves and places to find useful items. You can use these useful items for building your playable character with weapons, magic, armor, talismans, and buffs. Elden ring is known for bosses as there are unique 238 bosses and 15 main bosses (including DLC). Elden ring, a soulsborne game, means that the bosses are challenging and hard, especially the main bosses. After defeating some bosses, you gain access to the DLC, Shadow of the Erdtree, which is a whole new, different area set in the Realm of Shadow. This DLC is filled with different places, harder bosses and the hardest boss made in the soulsborne series.

My favorite character, Radahn Consort of Miquella, is the last and final boss of the DLC, this boss is probably the hardest boss made in the whole soulsborne series of Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Demon Souls and Sekiro.

  • The reason he is the hardest is because, he has many fast and hard to dodge attacks, one of his attacks, is where he goes up into the air and slams his swords with an after-image on the ground for 5 times and then releasing a giant light that almost instakills. Even streamers like Kai Cenat, took over 99 hours, while it took me a week to finish the whole DLC.
  • The story behind this character is that Miquella, a demigod and his consort, admired Radahn's strength, kindness and strong moral character, made Miquella thought that Radahn would help create an age of compassion. Then, Radahn was cursed with the Scarlet Rot, a disease that slowly eats away at his body and mind. After that, he is defeated by the player, the Tarnished, and is later revived inside the DLC, the Shadow of the Erdtree.
  • I also like that his boss design as his gold armor and his two greatswords look good. His boss arena also has to be my favourite as it is an arena covered in weapons and at the end is a giant gateway.
